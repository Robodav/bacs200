Amusements

Reddit

Reddit is a social news aggregation site that allows users to discuss a variety of topics, including news, professions, hobbies, and more.

VRV

A premium video streaming site created by the owners of Crunchyroll. It primarily hosts anime and related content, while also providing access to various films and even retro Nickelodeon shows.

Twitch

A livestreaming site with a focus on video games and esports. It provides creators with a streamlined interface for interaction with viewers, while also providing a wide selection of games to spectate.
